About brand

Concrete.no is a small young startup created in this difficult times based in Latvia. My name is Victoria, i decided to create this brand while i was on self-isolation. I am plant addicted person and for me it was always a problem to find a perfect pot for my plants, so i decided that easier is to create pots by myself!

I noticed that people are also interested in products i am making, there were so exicted about idea that every product is unique by their shape, color and appearance, after a lot of requests from my friends and family i decided to share my knowledge and products with my lovely customers!

This is how i started my exciting journey! I would be very happy to know You better!

Any questions?

What is your return policy?

I accept returns. In order to return, you have to contact me first.

While returning the item, the buyer is responsible for return shipping costs. If the item is returned damaged ater the delivery, the buyer is responsible for any loss in value.

Can I order online?

Yes! You can make an order via DM on my Instagram or order by writing a message through email: concrete.no@gmail.com

Is it eco friendly?

The products are made from long-lasting materials (concrete, jesmonite), which makes them reusable as well. The products are all produced in an ethical way.

Can I order products with custom design?

Yes! While ordering the products you can always have a custom order, but keep in mind that two similar designs are not possible, as all of the products are unique. In any way, I am always happy to combine your ideas with my passion.
